About Garden City

Ceebros Garden City, located in Sholinganallur, Chennai, is a luxury residential project offering premium 2BHK, 3BHK, and 4BHK apartments.

Project includes amenities such as a swimming pool, gym, children’s play area, landscaped gardens, and more.
